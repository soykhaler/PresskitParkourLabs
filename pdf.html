Welcome to Parkour Labs, a first-person parkour challenge built entirely around skill, precision, and flow. Face the ultimate movement test across 60 demanding levels, where mastering momentum, timing, and control is the only way forward.

This is a pure skill-based experience. The rules are simple: double jump and dash are all the tools you have. The objective is just as clear — reach the end without falling.

Experience

Set within surreal neon environments inspired by retro and synth-era aesthetics, Parkour Labs blends abstract architecture with fast, fluid movement. Sliding down ramps, chaining jumps, and maintaining momentum becomes a constant test of mastery, where learning the flow of each level is key to success.

This is a game about responsibility and improvement. There are no upgrades, no shortcuts, and no excuses — only your skill.

Key Features

  • Pure skill-based gameplay with no upgrades or randomness.
  • 60 handcrafted levels designed to demand precision and flow.
  • Highly optimized performance with excellent framerate across all platforms.
  • Split-screen multiplayer to challenge friends locally.
  • Online multiplayer on PC via Steam Remote Play Together.
  • Steam Deck verified for a smooth experience anywhere.
