Introduction

Welcome to Parkour Labs. Prepare yourself for a first-person platforming challenge focused entirely on precision, timing, and control. Test your mastery of movement across 60 demanding levels designed to push your skills to the limit.

The rules are simple: double jump and dash are the only mechanics at your disposal. Your objective is equally clear — reach the end of each level without falling. Every mistake is the result of your own decisions, and every success is earned through practice and consistency.

Parkour Labs is built around responsibility and improvement. There are no upgrades, no shortcuts, and no progression systems that alter gameplay. Mastery comes solely from learning the flow of each level and refining your execution.

About the Developer

SoyKhaler is an independent solo developer based in Spain, focused on creating mechanically precise, performance-oriented games. Parkour Labs is a long-term personal project developed with an emphasis on control, responsiveness, and platform optimization.

About Dojo System

Dojo System S.L. is a Spanish publishing and services agency for independent video games. Based in Madrid, Dojo System supports studios worldwide in strategy, communication, and international distribution for PC and consoles.
